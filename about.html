About G Jordan & G73

G Jordan is an artist from Kalamazoo, MI, born April 28th, 2001 (23 years old). Through a vision fueled by hard work and a deep connection to music as therapy, G Jordan aspires to create something truly special within the music industry.

G73 represents more than just music — it's the dream of building a powerful label for independent and underground artists, not only across the digital landscape but within live performances as well.

From a young age, G Jordan found the workings of the world fascinating, always drawn to reverse engineering and innovation. Now, that passion is focused on creating influence and impact through music, entrepreneurship, and authentic self-expression.

It's not just about beats or lyrics — it's about loving what you do, working hard, and passionately chasing your goals. G73 is more than a brand; it's a movement to inspire creators worldwide.
